My Research

As a population geneticist, my scientific passion lies in understanding natural populations and their evolutionary dynamics. My research focuses on medical entomology, with a particular commitment to bridging the gap between basic science and applied vector control. I strongly believe that effective vector control must be informed by robust scientific evidence, and that translating research into practical, field-relevant solutions is imperative.
